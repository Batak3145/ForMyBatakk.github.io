To My Sweetheart Batak💖

You're the reason my cheeks always glow,

The spark in my smile you may not know.

With every laugh and every wink,

You make me blush before I blink.

When you hold me close and spin me round,

My feet forget to touch the ground.

You mess my hair, you tease, you grin,

And that's when butterflies begin. 🦋

You play too much, you never stop,

You boop my nose, you steal my pop.

You joke and run, then pull me near

And suddenly, I feel no fear.

You make the world feel soft and sweet,

Like cozy socks and bedtime treats. 🍭

Like puppy cuddles, candy skies,

And love that sparkles in your eyes. ✨

So here’s a poem, just for you,

To say “I love you” through and through.

You’re not just love..you’re joy, you’re grace,

You, my love, are my happy place. 💕

From batak💕
